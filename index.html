Hogwarts houses

Around their eleventh birthday, the British witches and wizards receive, if they have shown magical abailities, their admission letter from te school for School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Hogwarts. On September first, After having taken the Hogwarts Express at 11am precisely on King's cross 9 3/4' platform, they arrive in the beautiful castle


Before their first dinner, they must be sorted out by the Sorting Hat into one of the different Hogwarts named after the 4 founders of the school and most amazing wizards and witches of their time: Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff or Salazar Slytherin. In Harry Potter's first year, 40 students, 10 per houses, were sorted out. It means that Hogwarts has around 280 students in each year. This might becomes very hard to remember and that's why, in this first visualisation, we let you explore the Hogwarts houses and the characters that belongs to the, for the main characters in the Harry Potter saga.

Exploring the association groups

Have you ever wonder in which group were both Fred Weasley and Angeline Johnson before getting married ? Or can you name all the member of the Order of the Phoenix during the first war against Vold.... hem, He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named ? Well, in Harry Potter, as in every such saga, spanning several years and a lot of different situations, keeping tracks of every group of characters and who are in, is really hard as they contain complex social relations that are evolving over time. To help you understand them, we give you the possibility to interactively explore a subset of the different Harry Potter association groups, their members and the different association in common between multiple characters. Note that for visualisation purposes, we did not include Hogwarts houses since they were already shown in a former visualisations.

How to use the graph in the next slide
  • Click on node: select character and neighbours.
  • Double click on node: display all characters information.
  • Click on background: deselect all nodes.
  • Click on multiple node: display the association in common.
  • Click on menu on the left: hide or show the association.

    Spell Launched per book

    What defines magic in the Harry Potter World ? The wand. Every witch and wizard should have one in order to learn how to control magic. This learning process comes along with a lot of different spells along the way. As you have previously seen, spells can be of any type and there is a huge amount of them. Ever wondered how many times and at which point in time in the saga each spell has been casted ? The next visualization will exactly answer this question. You will be able to see a timeline with all the times each of 20 most used spells has been casted during the Harry Potter saga. By hovering the different circles, you will be able to see the context in which the spell has been casted. Furthermore, the books have been color-coded. To be more precise on which circle to hover, you can click and drag to zoom inside the timeline.
